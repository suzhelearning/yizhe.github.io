Robotics

Dexterous manipulation, humanoid systems, and learning-based robot deployment.

I work on robotics algorithms that connect learning systems with real hardware. My current focus is dexterous hand control at Current Robotics. On LinkedIn I use the name Ethan Sun and describe my background as an algorithm engineer familiar with mechanical and electronic systems.

Research and Engineering Themes

Dexterous Hands

Algorithms for manipulation with high-dimensional hands, with attention to policy robustness, retargeting, control interfaces, and real-world execution.

Robot Deployment

Deployment workflows for learning-based manipulation systems, including task adaptation, inference integration, timing checks, and hardware validation.

Humanoid Data Collection

My HumanoidExo work studies wearable exoskeleton data collection for whole-body humanoid manipulation.
