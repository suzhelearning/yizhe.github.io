  1. ICRA
    HumanoidExo: Scalable Whole-Body Humanoid Manipulation via Wearable Exoskeleton
    Rui Zhong, Yizhe Sun, Junjie Wen, Jinming Li, Chuang Cheng, Wei Dai, Zhiwen Zeng, Huimin Lu, Yichen Zhu, and Yi Xu
    ICRA 2026, Oct 2025
    Abs arXiv PDF Website Video

    Humanoid policy learning is bottlenecked by the scarcity of large-scale, diverse training data. HumanoidExo is a wearable exoskeleton system that transfers human motion to whole-body humanoid data, minimizing the embodiment gap between human demonstrators and the robot. Combining a Vision-Language-Action model with reinforcement learning, it enables complex manipulation and locomotion: tabletop manipulation, manipulation with standing-squatting movements, and full-body manipulation, generalizing to novel environments with minimal real-robot data and acquiring new skills such as walking from exoskeleton-collected data alone.
